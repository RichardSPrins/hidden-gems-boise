Every dollar you spend at a locally owned business has an outsized impact on your community. Studies consistently show that for every $100 spent at a local business, significantly more stays in the local economy compared to spending at a national chain.

## 1. Your Money Stays in the Treasure Valley

When you buy from a locally owned business in Boise, that owner pays local employees, buys from local suppliers, and reinvests in the community. That cycle compounds.

## 2. You Get Something Unique

Local businesses offer products and experiences you simply can't find anywhere else. The character of a neighborhood is built by its independent shops, restaurants, and studios.

## 3. You're Supporting Real People

Behind every local business is a family, a story, and a dream. When you walk through that door, you're not just making a transaction — you're part of someone's livelihood.

## 4. Better Service, Every Time

Independent business owners have a direct stake in your experience. They're not reading from a script or hitting KPIs — they care because it's theirs.

## 5. It Makes Boise More Boise

The reason people love living here is because of the independent character of the city. That character is made and maintained one local business at a time.
